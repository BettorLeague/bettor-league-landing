BettorLeague Features

Live

Updated scores and live rankings after each goal. Do not miss a prognosis: receive a summary of your notifications every day by e-mail for the events you have selected.

Private Prognostic Contest

Want to play only with your friends, family or colleagues ? Don't worry, we thought about it also. Create your private contest, and be the captain of your own league.

Interactive rankings

Play against people and have a ranking. Every match will give score depending of your prognose and the issue of the match. Show the world who's the best bettor.

Live Chat Services

For each competition, there will be an available chat services. Means that whenever you start or join a contest, there will a chat service only for the contest. Interesting huh ?

Many Competitions Available

Apart form the 5 five major leagues (Ligue1, Liga, Premier League, Bundesliga and Seria A), we also propose to our Bettors, competitions like Eredivisie, Liga NOS and one of the most prestigious competitions : UEFA Champions League.
